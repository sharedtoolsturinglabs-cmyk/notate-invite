Notate — Privacy Policy

Effective date: 4 June 2026 · Contact: privacy@notate.live

Notate is a Chrome extension that lets you pin a comment and a screenshot to any web page and export it to an issue tracker (Linear, Jira, GitLab, Slack, or Notion). This policy explains what Notate collects, why, where it goes, and your choices. We do not sell your data or use it for advertising.

1. Local-first by default

Until you sign in and join a cloud workspace, your comments, screenshots, page snapshots, and the page URLs you pin to are stored only on your device in the browser's extension storage. Nothing in this category leaves your browser.

2. What we collect, and when

Screenshots and snapshots capture what is visible on the page you choose to annotate. Only capture pages you are comfortable sharing with your workspace. Email addresses are masked and URL query strings removed before any diagnostic is transmitted.

3. Who we share with (sub-processors)

  • Supabase — database, authentication, and private file storage for your cloud workspace.
  • Sentry — crash/error reporting (only after consent; data is scrubbed of emails and query strings).
  • The issue tracker you choose (Linear / Jira / GitLab / Slack / Notion) — receives the comment and screenshot only at the moment you click export.

We do not share your data with anyone else, and we never sell it.

4. Limited Use (Google API & browsing data)

Notate's use of information received from Google APIs, and of any browsing data, adheres to the Chrome Web Store User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements. We do not transfer, use, or sell this data for personalized advertising, to data brokers, or to determine creditworthiness or for lending purposes. Data is used solely to provide the feedback-and-export feature you invoke.

5. Diagnostics consent

Crash and error diagnostics are off until you consent. Consent is recorded when you sign in (the sign-in screen states that continuing accepts these terms). You can disable diagnostics at any time from the extension's settings or by signing out.

6. Storage, retention & deletion

  • Local data is removed when you uninstall the extension or clear the comment.
  • Cloud data is deleted when you delete the comment or the workspace. To request full deletion of your account data, email privacy@notate.live.
  • Access is restricted by Postgres Row-Level Security: only members of a workspace can read its comments; screenshots are served via short-lived signed URLs.

7. Security

All network traffic uses HTTPS. OAuth client secrets are held server-side and never ship in the extension. Access tokens are stored in your browser's extension-only storage, which web pages cannot read.

8. Children

Notate is not directed to children under 13 and we do not knowingly collect their data.

9. Changes

We will update this page and revise the effective date for material changes.
